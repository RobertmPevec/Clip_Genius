Design Specifications for

CLIP GENIUS: NPL UNIT

Introduction

In today’s fast-paced digital world, videos have become a primary medium for sharing information, ideas, and stories. However, with the increasing volume of video content, identifying the most important and relevant moments can be a time-consuming and challenging task. Our software is designed to simplify this process by leveraging advanced technology to analyze and highlight the key parts of a video based on what’s being said. Think of it as having an intelligent assistant that listens to your video and guides you directly to the moments that matter most.

How It Works

  1. Audio Extraction: We begin by isolating the audio track from your video, preparing it for analysis. This step is akin to extracting the soundtrack from a movie, ensuring the audio is ready for further processing.

  2. Chunk Splitting: To handle lengthy audio files, we divide the audio into smaller, manageable segments. This approach is similar to breaking a large book into chapters, making it easier to process and analyze.

  3. Speech-to-Text Transcription: Using NVIDIA’s NeMo ASR model, we convert the spoken words in each audio segment into text. This powerful tool acts like a virtual transcriber, accurately turning speech into written form.

  4. Semantic Analysis: Finally, we analyze the transcribed text to identify the most important parts. By comparing the text against predefined topics or keywords, such as “machine learning,” we pinpoint the segments that are most relevant to your needs.

Application Overview

The application is designed to automatically generate video highlights from a given video file. It uses advanced technologies like speech recognition, semantic analysis, and video processing to identify and extract the most relevant segments of the video. The highlights are then compiled into a shorter, engaging video.

Technologies Used

  • Languages: Python.

  • Libraries:

    • FFmpeg for audio/video processing.

    • NVIDIA NeMo for speech-to-text transcription.

    • Sentence Transformers for text embeddings.

    • FAISS for similarity search.

  • Parallel Processing: Concurrent.futures for efficient chunk processing.

Performance Considerations

  • Parallel Processing: Used multi-threading to speed up transcription and embedding tasks.

  • Efficient Storage: Deletes temporary files (e.g., audio chunks) after processing to save disk space.

Tests

  • Test Case: Verify that the application successfully converts a 15-minute video into a 10-minute highlights video by removing unnecessary parts.

  • Steps:

    • Upload a 15-minute video file.

  • Expected Result: The output video is of reduced length, containing only the most relevant segments from the original video.

  • Outcome: Successfully converted a 15-minute video into a 10-minute highlights video after removing unnecessary parts. The application accurately identified and retained the key moments while discarding less relevant content.

Future Enhancements

  • Customizable Highlight Length: Allow users to specify the desired length of the highlights video.

  • Multi-Language Support: Extend transcription capabilities to support multiple languages.

  • Cloud Integration: Enable processing of videos stored in cloud storage (e.g., Google Drive, Dropbox).
